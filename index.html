🤟👌 Indian Sign Language Recognition 👋✊

⚡ Real-time Detection 🔊 Voice Output 🤖 AI Powered

Breaking Communication Barriers with AI

Indian Sign Language (ISL) is used by hearing-impaired individuals to communicate. However, many people do not understand sign language, creating a communication gap.

This project uses Artificial Intelligence to recognize hand gestures in real-time and convert them into text and speech, helping bridge this gap.
